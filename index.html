I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in George W. Woodruff School of Mechanical Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, after obtaining my PhD degree in solid mechanics from Brown University. My research interest resides in designing more mechanically resilient materials and structures for aerospace and energy applications while enabling more socioresilient utilization of these materials throughout their life cycles. Concurrently with this major research thrust, I am committed to constructing a machine learning-enabled open-access platform for materials characterization. (CV)
